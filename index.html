Yu-Wen (Wendy) Lin

I am a second year Ph.D. student in the Department of Electrical Engineering and Computer Sciences at UC Berkeley mentored by Prof. Costas Spanos. I received B.S. in Electrical Engineering and Computer Engineering at Georgia Tech in 2018.

My research interests lie broadly in the span of optimization, machine learning, control systems and their applications to smart buildings. My recent work focuses on modeling human hysteresis, generative models, and applying deep reinforcement learning to program synthesis.
